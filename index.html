Machine learning has a lot to offer in the fields of medicine. So we decided to make a small demonstration of it using 3 of most common machine learning algorithms and then putting them to the test for detecting breast tissue cancer. The following steps will explain to you our data analysis process

STEP 1

Existing data is collected containing anonymous data about tumours found in various patients

STEP 2

Data is cleaned and redundant tuples are dropped from dataset.

STEP 3

Data is divided into 2 sets for training and testing machine learning models.

STEP 4

Data is fed to ML models and their results are obtained

FINISH

Performance reports of different models are compared and one model is finalised
